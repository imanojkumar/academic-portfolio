Operations Analytics

Operations Analytics

An applied course on process analysis, capacity, forecasting, inventory, scheduling and optimisation for operational performance.

Programme level
Graduate / Postgraduate
Duration
12 weeks
Delivery format
Classroom / Online / Hybrid
Credits
3
Intended audience
Operations, supply-chain, engineering management and business analytics students and professionals.
Prerequisites
Basic quantitative methods and spreadsheet modelling.

Learning outcomes

  • Measure and diagnose operational performance.
  • Develop demand and workload forecasts.
  • Analyse capacity, queues, inventory and service levels.
  • Formulate scheduling and optimisation decisions.
  • Recommend operational improvements using analytical evidence.

Module structure

  1. Operational performance and process data — Flow, capacity, productivity and service measures.
  2. Forecasting for operations — Demand, workload and forecast evaluation.
  3. Capacity, queues and service — Congestion, resource decisions and service trade-offs.
  4. Inventory and scheduling analytics — Policies, priorities and resource allocation.
  5. Optimisation and implementation — Models, scenarios and operational adoption.

Assessment methods

  • Operations modelling exercises — 30%
  • Process improvement case — 30%
  • Operations analytics project — 40%
