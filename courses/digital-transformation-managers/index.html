Digital Transformation for Managers

Digital Transformation for Managers

A management course on digital strategy, operating-model transformation, technology choices, organisational change and value delivery.

Programme level
Graduate / Postgraduate
Duration
10 weeks
Delivery format
Classroom / Online / Hybrid / Executive Workshop
Credits
3
Intended audience
Managers, technical leaders, consultants and graduate students responsible for digital change.
Prerequisites
Professional or academic exposure to organisational processes is helpful.

Learning outcomes

  • Assess digital pressures and strategic opportunities.
  • Define a transformation ambition and portfolio of initiatives.
  • Evaluate operating-model and technology implications.
  • Plan governance, change and capability development.
  • Create a value-focused transformation roadmap.

Module structure

  1. Digital strategy and competitive change — Business models, ecosystems and strategic priorities.
  2. Customer, process and data transformation — Experience, operations and enterprise information.
  3. Technology and operating-model choices — Platforms, architecture, sourcing and ways of working.
  4. Leading organisational change — Leadership, capabilities, culture and adoption.
  5. Transformation portfolio and value delivery — Roadmaps, governance, measures and benefits.

Assessment methods

  • Digital maturity assessment — 25%
  • Transformation case analysis — 30%
  • Transformation roadmap — 45%
