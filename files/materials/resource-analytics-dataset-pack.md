# Business Analytics Dataset Pack

**Associated course:** Business Analytics  
**Format:** Dataset guide  
**Suggested duration:** Self-paced

## Abstract

A structured set of classroom dataset descriptions and activity prompts for applied business analysis.

## Purpose

A guide to selecting and preparing datasets for business analytics teaching, together with the structure of the accompanying dataset pack and guidance on adapting it to a particular cohort.

## What this resource contains

- Dataset selection criteria for teaching, including realistic imperfection
- Structure and provenance of each dataset in the pack
- Known data quality issues, retained deliberately for teaching purposes
- Suggested exercises by analytical technique
- Guidance on substituting institution-specific or sector-specific data

## How to use it

The datasets retain quality problems on purpose. Missing values, inconsistent coding and ambiguous field definitions are part of the teaching material, not defects to be corrected before use.

Where a cohort has a strong sector orientation, substituting a comparable sector dataset generally improves engagement. The selection criteria are provided so that substitutions preserve the teaching properties of the original.

## Adaptation

The resource is intended to be adapted. Where it is adapted for a specific institution or cohort, the adaptation should preserve the sequence of questions rather than the particular wording.
