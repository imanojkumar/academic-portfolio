# AI Opportunity Assessment Template

**Associated course:** AI for Business  
**Format:** Template  
**Suggested duration:** Workshop resource

## Abstract

A reusable worksheet for evaluating business value, feasibility, readiness and implementation considerations.

## Purpose

A structured template for assessing a proposed AI application before commitment. It is designed to surface the questions that are most often left unasked until implementation is already under way.

## What this resource contains

- Decision definition - what decision or task the application is intended to change
- Value basis - how benefit would be observed, and by whom
- Data requirements - what is needed, what exists, and the gap between them
- Feasibility assessment - technical, organisational and regulatory
- Risk and failure modes - including the consequence of a confident wrong answer
- Evidence gate - what would have to be demonstrated before further commitment

## How to use it

Use as a workshop instrument with a mixed group of business and technical participants. The template works best when completed in the room rather than circulated for individual completion, because the disagreements it surfaces are the substance of the exercise.

Allow approximately ninety minutes for a single opportunity. Groups that finish substantially faster have usually not tested the value basis.

## Adaptation

The resource is intended to be adapted. Where it is adapted for a specific institution or cohort, the adaptation should preserve the sequence of questions rather than the particular wording.
