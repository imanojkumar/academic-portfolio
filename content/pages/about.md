# About this portfolio

This website presents a focused collection of graduate and postgraduate courses for Business Management and Engineering. The portfolio brings together course structures, case studies, presentations, applied projects and teaching resources at the intersection of management, analytics, artificial intelligence, digital transformation, operations and supply chains.

## Teaching orientation

The materials are designed around practical questions faced by managers, engineers and organisations. Course pages emphasise the intended audience, learning outcomes, module structure, assessment approach and related resources so that visitors can quickly understand how each course can be delivered or adapted.

## Professional context

Manoj Kumar combines extensive industry experience with academic teaching and course development. His work connects business transformation, analytics, artificial intelligence, engineering management and operational improvement.
